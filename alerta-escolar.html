Alerta Escolar

Real-time notification system that alerts parents when their children enter or exit school. Currently serving 2,000+ users at Secundaria Lázaro Cárdenas in Tijuana, BC.

Flutter · Dart · Supabase · Firebase · PostgreSQL

How It Works

Students scan their PVC ID credentials—printed in-house and linked to the platform via each student matrícula—when entering or leaving school. Parents instantly receive a push notification with the timestamp and access type.

QR code scanning at school entrance Instant push notifications to parents Complete attendance history Schedule and announcements access

Alerta Escolar App Flow

A streamlined experience that guides school staff, parents, and scanners through the same platform—from administrative control to instant notifications on every check-in.

Administrative Panel

Mobile app dashboard that lets school staff manage students, track attendance, configure schedules, and send communications right from their devices.

  • Student directory with filters
  • Real-time attendance tracking
  • Schedule management by group
  • Mass announcement system
  • Role-based access control

Parent Mobile App

iOS and Android app providing parents with real-time visibility into their children's school attendance and activities.

  • Entry/exit notifications
  • Weekly class schedules
  • School announcements
  • Multiple children support
  • Family contact management

QR Scanner Interface

Optimized scanning interface designed for high-volume use with professional QR readers. Provides instant feedback and triggers notifications automatically.

  • Compatible with Steren COM-5970
  • Live scanning feedback
  • Automatic notification trigger
  • Offline queue support

Notification System

Firebase Cloud Messaging delivers instant notifications to parents the moment their child scans their credential at the school entrance.

  • Sub-second delivery latency
  • Entry and exit alerts
  • School-wide announcements
  • Emergency notifications

Technical Stack

Built with Flutter for cross-platform development, Supabase for real-time backend, Firebase for push notifications, and PostgreSQL for data storage.
